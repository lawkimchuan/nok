Welcome to our design agency, we are a strategic design studio based in Singapore and Taipei.

We believe that design is about shaping the world we live in for the better. That is why we use a twin approach of design fiction and creative thinking, to create compelling visions of the future that inspire and inform our partners.

Founded in 2007, we specialize in futures thinking; design research and strategy, brand design and storytelling, experience design and industrial design. We are committed to building strong and collaborative relationships with our clients, just like next of kin. Whether you are looking to create a new brand, launch a new product, or shape your desired future, we are here to help you achieve your goals.

If you’re interested in working with us to create a better tomorrow, we would love to hear from you!
